How to Request Account Deletion

We respect your right to delete your account and personal data from our system. Follow the steps below to submit a deletion request.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Send an email to our privacy team at privacy@digitalvalue.co.th
  2. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your full name
    • Email address used to register in FITT BSA app
    • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - FITT BSA"
    • Reason for deletion (optional)
  3. Verify your identity - We may ask for additional verification to ensure the security of your account
  4. Wait for confirmation - You will receive a confirmation email within 3 business days
  5. Account deletion - Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our system:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profile photo
  • Attendance Records: All check-in/check-out history
  • Stock Counting Records: All product counting submissions
  • Delivery Records: All delivery receipt records
  • Photos: All uploaded images
  • Location Data: All GPS coordinates from check-ins
  • App Usage Data: Activity logs and preferences
