3. Information Collected With Permission

We collect the following information only with your explicit permission:

  • Purpose: Verify your presence at the workplace during check-in/check-out

  • Precision: Precise location (GPS coordinates)
  • Storage: Stored with your attendance records

Camera Access

  • Purpose: Take photos of products during stock counting and attendance verification
  • When Collected: Only when you use camera features
  • Storage: Photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage

Photo Library Access

  • Purpose: Select existing photos for product images
  • When Collected: Only when you choose to select photos
  • Storage: Selected photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Under Thai Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), you have the following rights:

  1. Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data
  2. Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  3. Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  4. Right to Restriction: Request limitation of data processing
  5. Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  6. Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  7. Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (may affect app functionality)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
