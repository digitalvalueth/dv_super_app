## Introduction

Welcome to FITT BSA ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how Digital Value Company Limited collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application FITT BSA (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when using our App:

| Data Type             | Purpose                                  | Required |
| --------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | -------- |
| **Name**              | Display your identity in the app         | Yes      |
| **Email Address**     | Account authentication and communication | Yes      |
| **Profile Photo**     | Display your profile picture             | No       |
| **Company/Branch ID** | Associate you with your workplace        | Yes      |

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

| Data Type              | Purpose                         |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Device Information** | App compatibility and debugging |
| **Usage Data**         | Improve app performance         |
| **Crash Reports**      | Fix bugs and improve stability  |

### 3. Information Collected With Permission

We collect the following information only with your explicit permission:

#### Location Data

- **Purpose**: Verify your presence at the workplace during check-in/check-out
- **When Collected**: Only when you use the check-in feature
- **Precision**: Precise location (GPS coordinates)
- **Storage**: Stored with your attendance records

#### Camera Access

- **Purpose**: Take photos of products during stock counting and attendance verification
- **When Collected**: Only when you use camera features
- **Storage**: Photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage

#### Photo Library Access

- **Purpose**: Select existing photos for product images
- **When Collected**: Only when you choose to select photos
- **Storage**: Selected photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Primary Uses

1. **Account Management**
   - Create and manage your user account
   - Authenticate your identity

2. **App Functionality**
   - Process stock counting records
   - Record attendance (check-in/check-out)
   - Track delivery receipts
   - Generate reports

3. **Communication**
   - Send important notifications about your work
   - Respond to your inquiries

### Secondary Uses

4. **Analytics and Improvements**
   - Analyze app usage to improve features
   - Monitor and fix bugs
   - Understand user behavior to enhance user experience

5. **Security**
   - Detect and prevent fraud
   - Protect against unauthorized access

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:
